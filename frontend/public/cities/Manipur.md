# Imphal

## Kangla Fort

Kangla Fort was the ancient seat of Manipur’s royal dynasty and remains one of the most historically important landmarks in the state.

### History

For centuries, Kangla served as the political and cultural center of the Meitei kingdom before British occupation during the colonial era.

### Why Visit

* Explore Manipur’s royal history
* Ancient fortifications and temples
* Cultural and archaeological significance
* Peaceful heritage complex

---

## Ima Keithel

Ima Keithel, also called the Women’s Market, is one of the largest markets in the world operated entirely by women.

### History

The market evolved from traditional female-led trade systems practiced in Manipuri society for generations.

### Why Visit

* Unique women-led marketplace
* Traditional handicrafts and textiles
* Local food and cultural experience
* Important symbol of Manipuri society

---

## Manipur State Museum

The museum preserves tribal heritage, royal artifacts, costumes, manuscripts, and traditional art.

### History

The museum was established to document and preserve the cultural identity of Manipur’s diverse communities.

### Why Visit

* Learn about Manipuri history and tribes
* Traditional costumes and weapons
* Educational tourism destination
* Cultural heritage exploration

# Loktak Lake

## Loktak Lake

Loktak Lake is the largest freshwater lake in Northeast India and is famous for its floating islands known as phumdis.

### History

The lake has supported fishing communities and local livelihoods for centuries and remains central to Manipur’s ecology and economy.

### Why Visit

* Unique floating island ecosystem
* Scenic boating experiences
* Birdwatching and photography
* One of India’s most distinctive lakes

---

## Keibul Lamjao National Park

Located within Loktak Lake, this is the world’s only floating national park.

### History

The park was created to protect the endangered Sangai deer, considered the state animal of Manipur.

### Why Visit

* Rare floating wildlife habitat
* Spot the endangered Sangai deer
* Eco-tourism and nature exploration
* Important biodiversity destination

---

## Sendra Island

Sendra offers panoramic viewpoints over Loktak Lake and surrounding floating landscapes.

### History

The island developed as a tourism point due to its elevated location and scenic lake views.

### Why Visit

* Best panoramic views of Loktak
* Sunrise and sunset photography
* Peaceful natural atmosphere
* Popular tourist viewpoint

# Moirang & INA

## INA Memorial Complex

The INA Memorial commemorates the Indian National Army and Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose.

### History

Moirang became historically important after the INA flag was first hoisted here during World War II in 1944.

### Why Visit

* Important Indian freedom movement landmark
* Historical museum and exhibits
* National heritage significance
* Educational tourism experience

---

## Loktak Nearby Region

The Moirang region connects closely with the landscapes around Loktak Lake.

### History

The surrounding settlements historically depended on fishing, agriculture, and lake trade.

### Why Visit

* Scenic lakeside environment
* Rural tourism experience
* Traditional local culture
* Photography opportunities

# Ukhrul & Shirui

## Shirui Hills

Shirui Hills are internationally known for the rare Shirui Lily flower found only in this region.

### History

The flower became scientifically recognized for its rarity and ecological importance in the Himalayan biodiversity zone.

### Why Visit

* Rare botanical attraction
* Scenic mountain landscapes
* Trekking and hiking opportunities
* Mist-covered hill scenery

---

## Shirui Lily Festival

The festival celebrates local culture, tourism, and the region’s ecological significance.

### History

The festival was introduced to promote sustainable tourism and preserve local traditions.

### Why Visit

* Cultural performances and music
* Experience tribal traditions
* Seasonal tourism attraction
* Local food and crafts

---

## Tangkhul Villages

Traditional Tangkhul Naga villages preserve indigenous architecture and customs.

### History

These settlements have historically maintained strong tribal traditions and agricultural lifestyles.

### Why Visit

* Tribal cultural tourism
* Traditional village experiences
* Handicrafts and local cuisine
* Authentic rural landscapes

# Tamenglong

## Barak River

The Barak River flows through Tamenglong and supports the district’s forest ecosystem.

### History

The river has historically been important for transportation, fishing, and settlement development.

### Why Visit

* Scenic river valleys
* Nature photography
* Peaceful natural environment
* Adventure tourism potential

---

## Forest Waterfalls

Tamenglong is known for waterfalls hidden within dense tropical forests.

### History

The region’s heavy rainfall and mountainous terrain created multiple river and waterfall systems.

### Why Visit

* Offbeat waterfall exploration
* Untouched forest landscapes
* Eco-tourism opportunities
* Trekking and adventure travel

---

## Orange Orchards

Tamenglong is famous for orange cultivation across its hills.

### History

Orange farming became an important agricultural activity supporting local livelihoods.

### Why Visit

* Scenic agricultural landscapes
* Seasonal fruit tourism
* Rural countryside experience
* Photography opportunities

# Bishnupur & Andro

## Vishnu Temple, Bishnupur

This temple is one of the oldest surviving brick temples in Manipur.

### History

Built during the 15th century, the temple reflects the spread of Vaishnavite traditions in the kingdom.

### Why Visit

* Historic religious architecture
* Cultural and spiritual significance
* Heritage tourism attraction
* Ancient temple craftsmanship

---

## Andro Heritage Village

Andro is known for preserving traditional Manipuri village culture and pottery-making traditions.

### History

The village has long served as a cultural center for indigenous craftsmanship and traditional lifestyles.

### Why Visit

* Traditional pottery workshops
* Indigenous cultural museum
* Authentic village tourism
* Handicrafts and heritage experiences

---

## Doll and Cultural Museums

Andro contains museums preserving tribal and rural heritage collections.

### History

The museums were established to document local traditions and disappearing cultural practices.

### Why Visit

* Educational cultural tourism
* Traditional art and artifacts
* Insight into Manipuri communities
* Heritage preservation experience
